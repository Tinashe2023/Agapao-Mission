About Us

Agapao Mission is a non-governmental organization dedicated to providing humanitarian aid and support to communities across Africa. We are committed to serving with compassion and integrity, striving to make a positive impact on the lives of those we serve.

Our headquarters are located in West Virginia, USA, and we work with partners and volunteers on the ground in Africa to deliver effective and sustainable programs.
